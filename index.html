Goblin's Vault is an auction-house style trading game where you take control of a young, hard working goblin in the United Republic of Gobland. Scattered within the 5 lands of URG, vaults and quests are waiting to be opened with just the right combination of items and keys.

Get to know the citizens of URG while you buy and sell your way to affinity and riches in the legendary Nix City Market.

Buy and Sell.

At the core of Goblin's Vault is buying and selling items with other goblins. Nix City Market is busy 24/7 with deals.

Set alerts to be notified when items are listed

Tailor Your Goblin.

Wearable items from the 5 URG provinces allow you to make your goblin your own. Some item combinations can activate affinity bonuses within their province of origin.

Unlock Vaults. Complete Quests.

The vaults within each province require very specific items and keys to be opened. The contents could be mythic. Affinity can be earned by helping the goblins of URG with their quests. Gain enough affinity in a province and you could earn a noble title.
